Features

Our Bug Tracking System provides a centralized platform to manage and track bugs efficiently across your projects.

Key features include real-time bug reporting, assigning bugs to team members, tracking status updates, and generating detailed reports for management.

The system ensures no bug goes unnoticed and helps maintain high software quality while improving team collaboration.

About

This Bug Tracking System was developed to help software teams streamline their development process and maintain high-quality standards.

By offering a clear overview of all issues, developers can prioritize tasks, fix critical bugs faster, and reduce production delays.

The system is designed to be user-friendly, secure, and highly customizable to suit small to large-scale software projects.
